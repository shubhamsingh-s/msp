Our Roots

A Legacy of Pharmaceutical Excellence

Founded with a goal to make high-quality medical formulations accessible, Maa Sukriti Pharmaceuticals has grown into a leading name in the clinical supply industry. We support health systems with safe, affordable, and quality-controlled medicines.

We handle complete pharmaceutical processing from raw chemical sourcing to formulation, packaging, and regulatory documentation. By constantly investing in manufacturing technology and research, we maintain strict safety levels for all drug products.

Our Mission

Infrastructure

Modern Processing Units

Our advanced production complex features cleanroom divisions built to WHO specs, featuring low-tolerance air filtration systems (HEPA).

  • Separate Formulation areas for beta-lactams and non-beta-lactams.
  • Fully automated tablet compression and blister packaging units.
  • Strict HVAC humidity controls to prevent drug moisture degradation.
  • Modern testing lab containing HPLC, FTIR, and dissolution rate testing tools.
